As you step deeper inside, the sound of the heartbeat fill your ears. Suddenly, a deafening roar shakes the ground.

Before you can react, a massive appear from the shadows. It was the dragon! The beast leashes a fiery breath of flame to you. The heat forcing you to cover your face, you fall into darkness, with the sound of the dragon's roar.
