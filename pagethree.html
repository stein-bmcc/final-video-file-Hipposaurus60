A Hero's Choice

  • ↻ Start Over

You have accepted the King's mission. As you prepare to journey into the unknown, you are faced with a critical decision. Will you go to the cave, or will you enter the cave directly? Your choice will determine the fate of your journey.

  • Head to the fields

    The fields offer a chance to gather resources and perhaps find survivors. However, you might encounter hidden dangers, or discover allies who are trying to escape the chaos.
  • Enter the cave

    The cave offers shelter from the dragon’s fiery breath, but it’s dark and dangerous. There may be creatures lurking inside, or you could stumble upon hidden treasure. The cave might be the safest option, but it’s also the most uncertain.
